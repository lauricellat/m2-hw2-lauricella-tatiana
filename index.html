About Me

My name is Tatiana Lauricella and I live in Naples, FL. I live in sunny Naples with my husband, Dave. I attended the University of Florida from 2012-2017. During this time, I earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Special Education and a Master’s Degree in Early Childhood Education. After graduation I began a career as a 1st grade special education teacher mostly serving students with Autism. At the start of my 5th year teaching (and the peak of Covid-19) my career quickly shifted from a special education teacher to an e-learning teacher. Initially, the thought of having to recalibrate my well-prepared lesson plans and relationships with my students was difficult to manage. Having to totally alter not only the method that students retained knowledge, but the platform and resources they used to learn was an overwhelming. However, this unexpected turn created an imperative opportunity for me to reinvent my technical skills and redefine my professional path, which led me to find a new opportunity outside the classroom. I now hold the position of a Sr. Learning Experience Designer (LXD) developing online learning content for a global construction rental corporation. In my current role I create online courses in Storyline 360 & Rise 360, manage our online LMS, and dabble with website management. I have zero experience with coding, but I am excited to learn!
